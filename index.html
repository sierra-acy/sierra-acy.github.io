POUND Fit

I started taking POUND classes in 2018. I always hated exercising, but I really enjoyed POUND. In November 2020, I officially became a POUND Pro! See the details below for more information on POUND and how to join my classes!

What is it?

POUND is for everybody and every body, designed for all fitness levels! Channel your inner rockstar with this full body cardio-jam session inspired by the infectious, energizing, and sweat-dripping fun of playing the drums! Visit the POUND Fit website or the YouTube channel for more info and a sneak peak at what a POUND class looks like.

How do I join?

Classes are every Saturday at 11am PST. Click here to join our Zoom class!

Classes are donation-based and 100% of the proceeds (x3) will be donated to a charity. Check out the How do donations work? section for more details. This month, we're donating to National Indigenous Women's Resource Center. Click here to read about the charity and donate! The recommended donation is $5, but any amount counts.

What do I need?

  • A yoga mat to stand on. 2 yoga mats if available and if you will be Pounding on a surface you don’t want damaged (ex: hardwood floors)
  • Stick-like noisemakers! In POUND, we use Ripstix (those green drumsticks), that are weighted 1/4 pound each, but you can use anything else you have on hand! Drum sticks, cooking spoons, chopsticks, metal straws, your hands...
  • Water!!!
  • Shoes are optional
  • A device to see and hear me with!

How do dontions work?

This class is donation based. The recommended donation is $5, but any amount counts. In fact, every donation will be multiplied by 3!

How? Each month, we’ll donate class proceeds to a different charity. Then, I will match the total class donations for a month. Finally, my company will match my personal donation.

For example, if 5 people attend class every week for a month, paying $5 each class, the total class donations for the month are $100. I will match the $100 with my own personal donation. Then, my company will match my personal donation with another $100. That’s a total of $300 to a single charity in one month!

Have a charity you would like to see represented? I’m open to recommendations!

Find the link to the current donation campaign in the How do I join? section.
